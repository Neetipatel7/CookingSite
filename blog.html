Seriously Good Homemade Coleslaw


My favorite homemade coleslaw recipe made in no time with the most delicious creamy dressing. The coleslaw is quick and easy to make. It is even easy to make in advance. .

I made this coleslaw recipe for the first time, at my friend’s request, along with the first batch of Memphis Style Ribs we ever cooked. We all loved it so much; I wound up making this slaw twice more with each subsequent batch of veggie burgers. Updated to add: I’ve been making this coleslaw at least once a month for the past six years. Every time someone tastes it (especially if said person is not already a fan of coleslaw) they ask for the recipe. This is the coleslaw that you’ll want to serve all summer long. If you’ve never made coleslaw before, let me share a secret: it’s the easiest side dish in the world. .


Let me tell you, that homemade coleslaw is so much better than anything you can get from the deli section at your grocery store. Crunchy crisp shredded cabbage with creamy mayo and tangy mustard makes the ideal complement to so many savory main dishes.


INGREDIENTS
  1. 3 cups green cabbage (finely shredded)
  2. 2 cups purple cabbage (finely shredded)
  3. 1 cup carrots (finely shredded)
DIRECTIONS
  1. Combine all dressing ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Toss with cabbage & carrots. Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving to allow flavors to blend.
