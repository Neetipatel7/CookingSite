Welcome to my Website


Hello my name is Neeti, people come to my Kitchen website looking for recipes and help to get meals on the table with a minimum of fuss. There are over many vegetarian/ vegan recipes on this website, all made from scratch as simply as possible.


Whether you’re looking for an easy dinner, a fun snack, breakfast ideas, desserts, or a salad so good you’ll want to eat it every day, the perfect recipe is waiting here for you.


I love food. I love simple foods, gourmet foods, healthy foods, you name it. I love dark chocolate, giant salads, spicy green chile, chips and salsa, homemade ice cream, chocolate ice cream, and frozen yogurt.
