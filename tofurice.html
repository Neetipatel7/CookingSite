Vegan Tofu Rice Bowl


Ingredients for the Tofu
  1. 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  2. 1/4 cup water
  3. 1/4 cup tamari
  4. 2 tablespoons chili sauce
  5. 1 16-ounce package of tofu, cubed
Ingredients for the Bowl
  1. 2 cups of cucumber
  2. 1 bag of peas
  3. 1 tablespoon sesame or canola oil (or any oil)
  4. 3 cups cooked white rice
  5. 1/4 cup cilantro, minced
  6. 2 tablespoons mint, minced
  7. 3 tablespoons seasoned rice wine vinegar
  8. Sriracha (or other sauce)
  9. 1/4 cup peanuts, chopped (or any toppings you like)
DIRECTIONS
  1. Mix together all of the tofu ingredients, except the tofu itself, in a bowl or plastic bag. Add tofu to marinade mixture and set aside. Tofu should marinate for at least 1 hour, and up to 8 hours.

  2. Place carrots and cucumbers into two separate bowls Add 1 tablespoon if rice wine vinegar to the bowl with the cucumbers and toss to coat. Set aside for 1 or more hours.

  3. Add 1 tablespoon of sesame or canola oil to cast iron or non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Place marinated tofu in pan (but not the marinade). Cook tofu in the pan for 10 minutes until browned. Toss tofu every 2 minutes or so to brown on most sides.

  4. Once browned, pour the marinade over the tofu and cook for 1 additional minute, until all of the liquid has been absorbed. Remove from heat and set aside until ready to assemble bowls.

  5. Place 1/2 cup white rice in each bowl. To each bowl, add 1/4 cup each of tofu, carrots, and cucumbers. Top with cilantro, mint, and peanuts. Drizzle with sriracha and serve.
