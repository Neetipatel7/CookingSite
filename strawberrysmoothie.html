Frozen Strawberry Lemonade Smoothie


INGREDIENTS
  1. 1 handful of frozen strawberries
  2. 1/2 of a lemon a lemon juice
  3. about 3/4 cup of ice (preferable)
  4. 2 packets of Truvia (all natural sweetener)
  5. about 3/4 cup of water
DIRECTIONS
  1. Take frozen strawberries and ice put them in the blender
  2. add juice half of a lemon into the blender
  3. I add 2 packets of Truvia (or any of your choice sweetener) You could substitute 2 teaspoons of white sugar
  4. Then I add water until it almost covers the strawberries
  5. Adjust water and ice to your preferences on thickness and sweetness. Blend well.serve
